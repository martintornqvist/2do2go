It's easy!

You just have to click on the 'Make a list' button.

Then add the things you want to have on the list.

Once you hit the 'Share' button it creates an unique URL for you to share to anyone.

The viewer (who recieves the shared URL) is still able to edit and delete items from that specific list.


And the best part?

If you already shared the URL and want to add something to the list - go ahead!

It will update the viewers list automatically!
