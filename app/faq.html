It's easy!

  • Just head over to 'Register' and fill out the form, once registered you're redirected to your own dashboard.
  • At your dashboard you'll be able to create new lists, delete previous lists and edit or add items to your older ones that you want to use again.
  • The lists will be stored securely and are only accessible to you through your account UNLESS you hit the 'Share'-button which then creates an unique URL for you to share.
  • Note: These 'shared lists' have some limitations, the user cannot delete or add anything to it, it's only a view type of mode.

Head back home to login or signup!
